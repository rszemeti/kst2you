KST2YOU is a project by Robin Szemeti, G1YFG. It aims to provide a web-based interface to the KST service, accessible on any modern browser, regardless of the device. If you have a feature request or comments, please contact me via my email as published on QRZ.com.

Beacon data from UK Microwave Group / Beaconspot.uk

Beacon data is current data kindly supplied by Beaconspot, a website run by the UK Microwave group. If you enjoy microwave radio or would like to contribute to the upkeep of our servers, beacon network or just receive our monthly newsletter, please consider a subscription (just £6 a year at the time of writing) — every little helps! BeaconSpot and Microwavers.org appreciate your support.

Privacy: I do not store your passwords. These are only stored in your browser if you select "remember me". I do keep a log of callsigns that have used the service and any DXC cluster spots purely for statistics. I do not log your messages either sent or received. Since I started collecting numbers in late October 2023, there have been logins to the service and spots reported.

A basic write-up on the various functions and features may be found on my blog https://g1yfg.blogspot.com
