Microwavers.org

KST2YOU is a project by Robin Szemeti, G1YFG , it aims to provide a web-based interface to the KST service, accessible on any modern browser, regardless of the device. If you have feature request or comments, please contact me via my email as published on QRZ.com

Beacon data from UK Microwave Group / Beaconspot.uk

Beacon data is current data kindly supplied by Beaconspot, a website run by the UK Microwave group. If you enjoy microwave radio or would like to contribute to the upkeep of our servers, beacon network or just receive our monthly newsletter, please consider a  subscription (just £6 a year at the time of writing) every little helps!  BeaconSpot  and Microwavers.org appreciate your support.

Privacy: I do not store your passwords. These are only stored in your browser if you select "remember me". I do not currently store any logs of who is using the service nor the messages they send. I may at some point in the future keep a log of callsigns that have used the service, purely for statistics to see if it is being used by enough people to justify further development.  I do not log your messages either sent or received.

A basic write-up on the various function and features may be found on my blog https://g1yfg.blogspot.com
